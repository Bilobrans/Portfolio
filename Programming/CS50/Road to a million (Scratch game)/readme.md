The goal is simple: start with $0 and reach $1,000,000.

## **Key Features:**

Work Mechanics: Start by taking freelance Excel jobs to earn your first dollars.

Upgrades: Invest your earnings to increase profit margins and speed up your activities.

Risk vs. Reward: A random encounter offers a 49% chance to double your money or lose everything. Be careful: you can only accept this offer 3 times per run!

Winning: Reach the million-dollar mark to claim your trophy.
